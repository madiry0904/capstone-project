You Are Not Alone

Millions of people worldwide struggle with managing their mental health often due to the rise of technology, work and personal related stress, and just everyday life stressors. The CDC estimates that approximately 50% of all Americans are diagnosed with a mental illness or disorder at some point in their lives with anxiety disorders affecting approximately 40 million adults aged 18+.

Too often we ignore our mental health because we "don't have any time" but here at Mental Health Made Simple we tend to disagree. That's why we've designed a quick and easy mental health quiz to get you functioning at your optimal level!

This quiz was designed to help you take back control of your mental health in an easy and convenient way! All you have to do is enter a few simple questions to receive some quick and effective exercises that can strengthen your mental health and lower your stress either at your desk or on the go!
