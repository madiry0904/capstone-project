You Are Not Alone

Millions of people worldwide struggle with managing their Mental Health often due to the rise of technology, work and personal related stress, and just everyday life stressors. The CDC estimates that approximately 50% of all Americans are diagnosed with a mental illness or disorder at some point in their lives with anxiety disorders the most common affecting approximately 40 million adults aged 18+.

Here at Mental Health Made Simple we think this is a major problem! That's why we've designed a quick and easy mental health quiz to get you functioning at your optimal level!

This quiz was designed for you to take back control of your mental health and begin to manage your stress in an easy digestible way! Too often we ignore our mental health because we "don't have any time" but here at Mental Health Made Simple we tend to disagree. This quiz was designed to provide quick and effective exercises you can do anywhere and anytime! Simply go to the Quiz page or click the button above to get started!
